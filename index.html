About the Circle

The Music Education Editors' Circle connects editors and editorial board members working with music education journals and publications across regions and languages. We share news and opportunities, exchange editorial practice, and support sound, ethical scholarship in our field.

Most substantive discussion takes place in our scheduled meetings, including the editor session at the ISME World Conference in July 2026. Day to day, the Circle keeps in touch through a simple email announcements list.

Join the announcements list

Membership is open to current and incoming journal editors, associate and section editors, and editorial board members in music education. You can join with any email address — the list does not depend on any single email provider, so colleagues everywhere can take part.

The list is used mainly for announcements and general queries: calls for papers, special issues, editorial openings, and meeting notices.
